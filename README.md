# Tableau Portfolio — Tejamanikanta Gudla

## Projects

### 1. 311 City Services Analysis
Analysis of municipal 311 service request data identifying response time patterns, department bottlenecks, and geographic service disparities across city districts. Built an operational dashboard enabling city managers to monitor SLA compliance and resource allocation in real time.

**Key techniques:** LOD expressions, parameter-driven views, geographic mapping, trend forecasting
**Skills demonstrated:** Public sector analytics, KPI tracking, operational reporting

---

### 2. Agrofood Analytics
Exploration of global agrofood supply chain data covering production volumes, trade flows, and food security indicators across regions. Visualized seasonal production cycles and supply-demand imbalances to support strategic planning insights.

**Key techniques:** Dual-axis charts, reference bands, dynamic filters, time series decomposition
**Skills demonstrated:** Supply chain analytics, agricultural data, international trade analysis

---

### 3. Data Science Salaries 2024
Interactive dashboard analyzing 2024 data science compensation trends by role, experience level, company size, remote ratio, and geography. Enables job seekers and hiring managers to benchmark salaries across the global data market.

**Key techniques:** Heat maps, box plots, parameter controls, calculated salary bands
**Skills demonstrated:** Labor market analytics, compensation benchmarking, HR data visualization

---

### 4. Data Science Company Profiles
Comparative analysis of data science companies by size, industry, funding stage, and technical stack requirements. Dashboard surfaces which companies hire for specific skills (Python, SQL, ML, Cloud) and how requirements vary by company tier.

**Key techniques:** Treemaps, word clouds, stacked bar charts, cross-filtering
**Skills demonstrated:** Market research analytics, skills gap analysis, recruiting intelligence

---

### 5. HR Analytics
Workforce analytics dashboard tracking headcount trends, attrition risk, tenure distribution, department performance, and hiring pipeline metrics. Designed for HR leadership to identify retention risks and optimize workforce planning decisions.

**Key techniques:** Cohort analysis, attrition rate calculations, demographic breakdowns, rolling averages
**Skills demonstrated:** People analytics, HR KPI design, executive dashboard development

---

### 6. Human Development Index (HDI)
Global visualization of the United Nations Human Development Index across 190+ countries, analyzing the relationship between education, life expectancy, income, and development tier over time. Includes animated time-lapse views showing development trajectories by region.

**Key techniques:** Choropleth maps, animated time series, scatter plot matrices, parameter-driven drill-down
**Skills demonstrated:** Global data visualization, socioeconomic analysis, UN data integration

---

### 7. U.S. Dept. of Education — College Scorecard
Analysis of U.S. college and university data from the Department of Education Scorecard dataset — comparing graduation rates, student debt loads, post-graduation earnings, and institutional characteristics across 6,000+ institutions.

**Key techniques:** Scatter plots with clustering, reference lines, dynamic ranking, multi-level filtering
**Skills demonstrated:** Education analytics, public data analysis, consumer decision-support dashboards

---

### 8. U.S. Electric Vehicle Adoption
State-level analysis of electric vehicle registration trends across the United States, tracking adoption rates, infrastructure availability (charging stations), and demographic correlations. Includes forecasting views for market saturation projections.

**Key techniques:** Geographic mapping, trend forecasting, parameter-driven scenario analysis, dual-axis overlays
**Skills demonstrated:** Energy transition analytics, market forecasting, policy impact visualization
